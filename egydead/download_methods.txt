# EgyDead Download Methods

## 1. Server: تحميل متعدد (hglink.to)
**Method:**
1.  Navigate to the URL.
2.  Click the "Download" button (often blue or green).
3.  **Note:** This server is a "multi-host" redirector. It will usually redirect you to another file host (like DoodStream, Mixdrop, or Gofile). You will then need to follow the method for that specific host.

## 2. Server: EarnVids (dintezuvio.com)
**Method:**
1.  Navigate to the URL.
2.  Click the "Free Download" button (usually grey or blue).
3.  Wait for the countdown timer (often 30 seconds).
4.  Click "Create Download Link".
5.  Click "Click here to download" to start the file transfer.

## 3. Server: Mirrorace (mirrorace.org)
**Method:**
1.  Navigate to the URL.
2.  **Challenge:** This site is often protected by Cloudflare ("Verifying you are human"). You may need to solve a CAPTCHA.
3.  Once passed, you will see a list of mirrors (e.g., 1fichier, DoodStream).
4.  Click on a mirror link to be redirected to that host.
5.  Follow the method for the chosen host.

## 4. Server: DoodStream (dsvplay.com / doodstream.com)
**Method:**
1.  Navigate to the URL.
2.  Click the "Download Now" button (Blue button).
    *   *Note:* This often opens a pop-up ad. Close the pop-up and click again if necessary.
3.  Wait for the "High quality" (or sometimes "Original") link to appear.
4.  Click the "High quality" link.
5.  On the next page, click the "Download file" link to start the download.

## 5. Server: 1fichier (1fichier.com)
**Method:**
1.  Navigate to the URL.
2.  Click the "Download" button (Orange/Red button).
3.  Wait for the countdown timer (if you are a free user).
4.  Click the "Click here to download the file" link to start the download.
